#### (i) \\(\textrm\{rank\}(AQ)=\textrm\{rank\}(A)\\)
\\(\textrm\{rank\}A\equiv\textrm\{dim\}(\textrm\{Im\}(A))\\) holds, so we will show that
\\[\textrm\{Im\}(AQ)\equiv\textrm\{Im\}(A).\\]
