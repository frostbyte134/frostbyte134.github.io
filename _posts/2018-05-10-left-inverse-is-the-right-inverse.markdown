### Theorem:
\\[AB=I \Leftrightarrow BA=I\\]
1. \\(\rightarrow\\): Since \\(AB=I\\), \\(B=(BA)B\\).
Note from \\(AB=I\\) that \\(\textrm\{det\}A\neq 0, \textrm\{det\}B\neq 0\\)
